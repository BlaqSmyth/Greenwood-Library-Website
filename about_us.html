About Greenwood Community Library

Greenwood Community Library has been a cornerstone of the community since its establishment in 1901. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for education, inspiration, and connection for people of all ages.

Our History

Founded by local philanthropist John Greenwood, the library started with a collection of 1,000 books. Over the years, it has grown into a modern facility offering a wide range of services including digital resources, community programs, and special events.

Our Team

Our team is composed of dedicated professionals and volunteers who are passionate about serving the community. We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome.
